Get to Know Me

"Last Origin" is a strategy turn-based RPG game. The game is set in a world where the infestation of iron worms parasitic in the machinery, it has been 100 years since the extinction of human beings. The "artificial life bodies (バイオロイド)" made by humans still firmly believe that human beings who guide them exist in the world. Player will become the last human being as the hope of humanity, lead the artificial life forms of beautiful girls to rebuild human civilization against iron worms.
